Filmmaking

One of the hardest subjects to film in the world. This shot was prepared for Tha Powwow Association. This is when we first met our graphic designer Kira Murillo. You can see her dancing at 3:09.

4K and 1080p resolutions, Blu-ray Distribution

Our film process uses secret post productions techniques that allow us to use 1080 frame sizes up to 4K resolutions. Which means our products look great from mobile phones to IMAX.

Sound Production Value

This type of music is one of the most difficult types to produce. The loud booming drum typically takes over the entire recording leaving little to work with and smears spatiality. Using a rare and technically challenging recording technique called ambisonics, we were able to recreate the feeling of 'being there'. Just close your eyes. This recording method also allows us to use the same equipment and workflow from stereo through to 5.1 and 7.1 surround sound.
